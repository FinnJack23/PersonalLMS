# Judging Alignment

- **Technological Implementation:** deterministic readiness/import controls,
  SQLite state, provider-neutral routing, citations, and loopback UI.
- **Design:** five visible stages make approval, evidence, gaps, lesson, and
  mastery understandable in one short flow.
- **Potential Impact:** reduces hallucinated study guidance and makes messy
  personal archives actionable.
- **Quality of the Idea:** retrieval failure is honestly shown as a learner
  signal instead of silently filled with unsupported claims.
